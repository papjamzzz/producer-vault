Welcome to ProducerVault
⚡
It's already working.
ProducerVault is watching your Ableton folder right now. Every time you hit ⌘S in Ableton, your project is automatically backed up to your private cloud storage — no clicks, no thinking about it.
Watching: {{ watch_folder }}
Backs up .als, .wav, .aif, .aiff, .flac files on every save.
🧠
Every save is a version.
ProducerVault reads your .als files and extracts the BPM, key, track count, and plugins for every single save. So instead of a pile of mystery files, you get a full timeline of your session — exactly what it sounded like at 3am, last Tuesday, before you made that change.
Example: midnight_session.als · 128 BPM · F# Minor · 14 tracks · Serum, Massive
↩️
Restore any version in one click.
See the ↓ restore button next to every backup? Click it and that exact version of your file downloads immediately. Drop it back into your Ableton folder and you're back. No complicated recovery. No data loss.
Your files are in your own Backblaze B2 bucket. We never store or see your projects. If you cancel, your files stay in your bucket.
